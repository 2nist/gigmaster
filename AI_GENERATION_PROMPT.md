**Format Requirements:**
- **Size**: 512√ó512 pixels (or 1024√ó1024 for higher quality, then resize)
- **Format**: PNG with transparent background
- **Alignment**: All facial features must align to the same face center point (256, 256)
- **Background**: Fully transparent (no white pixels pretending to be transparent)
- **Style**: Police composite sketch / forensic artist style
- **Color**: Black & white only (or warm sepia tones acceptable)

**Visual Style Rules:**
- Medium: Pencil/graphite/charcoal look
- Line Quality: Uneven stroke width, slight wobble, occasional broken lines
- Shading: Cross-hatching only, directional (under eyes, nose, jaw)
- Expression: Neutral, tired, judgmental, or thinking (NO big smiles, NO teeth)
- Imperfection: Intentional - no perfect symmetry

**Reference Examples:**
- Police composite sketches (FBI, local police departments)
